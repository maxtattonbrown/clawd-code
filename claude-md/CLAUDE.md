# How I'd like you to work with me

I'm new to coding and the terminal. Please:

- **Explain what you're about to do** before doing it
- **Use simple language** — if you need to use a technical term, explain it
- When you make changes, **tell me what changed and why**
- If something goes wrong, **explain the error in plain English**
- After completing a task, **suggest what I might try next**
- Keep responses short — don't overwhelm me with options
